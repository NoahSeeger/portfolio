## What is Bruno?

Bruno is an iOS app for tracking body transformation. Take progress photos, organize them by date, and compare before/after shots to see your progress.

## Features

- **Progress Photos**: Take and store photos with timestamps
- **Comparison View**: Swipe to compare photos from different dates
- **Timeline**: See your transformation in chronological order
- **SwiftData**: Uses Apple's new SwiftData for persistent storage

## Why This App?

Everyone wants to track their fitness journey but existing apps are complicated or require subscriptions. Bruno is simple - just photos and comparison.

## Tech Stack

Built entirely with SwiftUI and Apple's new SwiftData framework for modern, performant data handling.
