## What is PicSwipe?

PicSwipe is an iOS app that makes gallery cleanup fun. Instead of manually selecting hundreds of photos to delete, you swipe through them like Tinder - left to delete, right to keep.

## The Problem

Every phone has thousands of photos. Most are duplicates, blurry, or just not worth keeping. But deleting them one by one is tedious and takes forever.

## The Solution

Swipe through your gallery in seconds:
- **Swipe right** = keep photo
- **Swipe left** = mark for deletion
- **Review** before deleting anything permanently

## Tech Stack

Built with SwiftUI and PhotosUI framework for native iOS performance. Uses PHPhoenix for safe photo handling.
